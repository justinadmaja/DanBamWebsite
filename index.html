We Are Danbam.

We are an up-and-coming Korean hip restaurant that is always experimenting and creating new and modern dishes, while staying true to our Korean heritage and recipe.

Currently, we have several branches all across South Korea and also two international branches, in Japan and Taiwan.

Our Menu Recommendations

Kimchi

One of the oldest and most popular dish in Korean cuisine, kimchi is a spicy and sour dish made up of fermented vegetables.

Bibimbap

A bowl of mixed ingredients including rice, namul (sautéed vegetables), mushrooms, beef, soy sauce, gochujang and fried egg.

Bulgogi

A juicy, savory dish of grilled marinated beef. Currently, it is the best selling dish in our restaurant.

Tteokbokki

Spicy Korean food made of cylindrical rice cakes, triangular fish cake, vegetables, and sweet red chili sauce.
